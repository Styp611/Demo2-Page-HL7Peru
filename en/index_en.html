We are pleased to announce the exciting opening of HL7 Peru.
We promote the promotion of data interoperability in the field of health in Peru.
¡We invite you to meet our new HL7 Peru board of directors for the period 2023-2027!
Our strong, united and collaborative team aims to drive the interoperability
and promote the correct use of the HL7 standard in Peru.
We work together to promote the integration and exchange of information in the field of health,
seeking to improve the quality and efficiency of systems and processes in the sector.
